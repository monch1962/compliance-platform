RegoHub

AU compliance checking as a service — no install required

RegoHub is the hosted API that powers cicd-gate's compliance checks. Point it at your infrastructure, get back ISM and Essential Eight violation reports — without installing conftest, OPA, or anything locally.

What you get

  • 55+ Rego policies mapped to Australian ISM and Essential Eight controls
  • Continuous compliance monitoring across your IaC
  • Framework-identified violations with tier labels (L1-L4)
  • Audit-ready export reports
  • Multi-cloud compliance packs (AWS, GCP, Azure)

How it works

Connect your infrastructure (CI/CD pipeline, Terraform plan, or cloud account). RegoHub evaluates it against our Rego policy library — the same policies that power the open-source cicd-gate CLI — and returns structured compliance reports with ISM and E8 framework references.

Open source foundation

Every RegoHub policy is available in the open-source compliance-platform repository. The hosted API adds continuous monitoring, team collaboration, audit exports, and enterprise support — built on the same trusted policy engine.
